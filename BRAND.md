**Voice Principles**

**01 Sharp, Not Clever**

We write with precision. Active verbs, concrete outcomes, zero filler.
We don't perform intelligence---we let the clarity of our thinking do
the work.

**02 Warm, Not Soft**

We care deeply about our clients' outcomes. That care shows up as
directness, not deference. If a direction doesn't serve your vision,
we'll tell you why and find a better path.

**03 Confident, Not Loud**

We don't oversell. We state what we do, show the evidence, and let
founders draw their own conclusions. The work speaks; we just make sure
it's heard.

**PART THREE**

**Usage Notes**

**Words We Use**

Build, engineer, curate, architect, signal, synthesize, sharpen,
command, partner, invest.

**Words We Don't**

Leverage, synergy, disrupt, ideate, holistic, bespoke (overused), guru,
rockstar, ninja, hack.

**Formatting Conventions**

The brand name is always written as Studio---E with an em dash (---),
never a hyphen (-) or en dash (--). In contexts where special characters
are unsupported, use Studio-E as a fallback.

**Headline Formula**

Lead with an outcome the founder cares about. Keep it under 10 words.
Use active voice. Avoid adjectives that could describe any agency
(innovative, cutting-edge, world-class).

**CTA Language**

Primary CTA: "Book a Discovery Call." Secondary CTAs per service:
"Inquire About \[Service Name\]." Never use "Get Started" or "Learn
More"---these are generic and dilute the premium positioning.
